Our Vision

To create a welcoming environment for moms to relax, enjoy a cup of tea, and connect. The hope is to give moms the opportunity to meet other moms and develop support in this season of motherhood.

Who can attend?

We welcome all moms who are currently pregnant or moms with their baby/toddler (0-3 years) who live in the Tokai area and its surroundings.

What to Expect

Safe, relaxed, and welcoming environment.
Friendly and supportive volunteers.
Tea, coffee, and biscuits.
Play area with toys and activities.
Occasional guest speakers.
